Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, billing, and using Sorn Services.

What is Sorn Services?

Sorn Services is a Windows desktop application that helps gamers optimize their PC settings for better performance and lower latency. It bundles system tuning presets, configuration tools, and performance utilities into a single, easy-to-use interface.

How is the product delivered?

Sorn Services is a digital product. Immediately after a successful payment, your license key and a secure download link are sent to the email address used at checkout. Most customers receive their order within 1–2 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via MoneyMotion and cryptocurrency. Both methods are fully secure — your financial details never touch our servers. Payment instructions are shown at checkout.

What is your refund policy?

We do not offer cash refunds. If you're unsatisfied with a product, open a ticket in our Discord — your current license will be revoked and you'll receive a replacement license for any other product of your choice for the same duration you originally purchased.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us by opening a ticket in our Discord community. We typically respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

What are the system requirements?

Sorn Services runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). You'll need administrator rights on your PC to apply system optimizations. A full list of requirements is included on each product page before checkout.

How does the license work?

Each purchase includes a personal license for one user, activated on a single PC. If you upgrade or change computers, just email support with your order number and we'll happily transfer the license to your new device.

Is my personal and payment data safe?

Yes. All purchases are processed securely through MoneyMotion or cryptocurrency — your payment details never touch our servers. We only collect the information needed to fulfil your order and provide support, as outlined in our Terms of Service.
